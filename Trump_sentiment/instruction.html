Overview

In this job, you will be presented with tweets that are about Donald Trump collected during the 2016 U.S. presidential debates. Review the tweets to determine the  sentiment so that we can have a greater understanding about the overall public sentiment toward the politician.

Steps

  1. Read the tweet.
  2. Determine the Twitter user's attitude expressed in the tweet: positive, neutral, or negative.  
  • NOTE: Make your decision based on the explicit expression. Do not infer or over interpret.
